What is PureGH2

The Motivation

We seek to help communities and businesses reduce their CO2 emissions (which cause the climate crisis), by replacing their fossil fuel based energy with an off-grid sustainable alternative that is a combination of micro-hydro based electricity production and green hydrogen based electricity storage

Button

Our Process

We have a structured process to Define, Measure, Analyse, Improve, Control and Optimise the implementation of an energy solution that is comprised of an integrated Micro-Hydro + Green Hydrogen platform which together provide 24x7x365 clean energy for a range of requirements.

Button

Your Benefits

Our customers get a clean energy platform that:

  • satisfies their electricity requirement
  • is low cost and environmentally friendly
  • can be a potential revenue generator (from excess Hydrogen production)
  • reduce and/or eliminate dependency on 'on-grid' fossil fuel energy (while having it as a back-up for your 'off-grid' solution)

Green Energy Platform

Micro-Hydro Electricity

Our PureGH2 vision is to produce GREEN HYDROGEN using no fossil fuel in the production method. We do this by utilising a ‘micro-hydro’ process to power the Electrolyzer that produces GreenHydrogen and then store this GH2 in a number of ways such that it can be used to provide power for many different needs.

Green Hydrogen Storage

We have a structured process to Define, Measure, Analyse, Improve, Control and Optimise the implementation of an energy solution that is comprised of an integrated Micro-Hydro Electricity production platform and a Green Hydrogen Electricity production/storage platform - which collaborate to provide 24x7x365 clean energy for many requirements.
