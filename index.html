What is PureGH2

The Motivation

We seek to help communities and businesses reduce their CO2 emissions (which cause the climate crisis), by replacing their fossil fuel based energy with an off-grid sustainable alternative that is a combination of micro-hydro based electricity production and green hydrogen based electricity storage

Our Process

We have a structured process to Define, Measure, Analyse, Improve, Control and Optimise the implementation of an energy solution that is comprised of an integrated Micro-Hydro + Green Hydrogen platform which together provide 24x7x365 clean energy for a range of requirements.

Green Energy Platform

Micro-Hydro Electricity

PureGH2 uses an off-grid platform to produce electricity in collaboration with a strategic partner to enable an eco-friendly way to harness energy from rivers and canals - as a standalone project or a cluster of turbines powering entire regions. A single turbine can generate from 15 to 70 kW of constant energy around the clock.

Green Hydrogen Storage

PureGH2 enables our clients to produce their own Green Hydrogen (GH2) using no fossil fuel in the production method. We do this by utilising a micro-hydro process to power the Electrolyzer that produces GH2. We then store this GH2 in a number of ways such that it can be used to provide power for many different needs.
